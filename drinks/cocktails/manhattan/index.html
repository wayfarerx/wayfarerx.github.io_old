  • 5 cl rye whiskey
  • 2 cl sweet vermouth
  • 1 dash Angostura bitters
  • 1 Maraschino cherry
Hardware required:
  • cocktail glass
  • ice
  • mixing glass
  • mixing spoon
  • strainer
Assembly procedure:
  1. Add the whiskey, vermouth and bitters to a mixing glass with ice and stir well.
  2. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass and garnish with the Maraschino cherry.
