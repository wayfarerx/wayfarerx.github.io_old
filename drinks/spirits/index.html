Gin

A liquor which derives its predominant flavour from juniper berries (Juniperus communis).

Rum

A distilled alcoholic beverage made from sugarcane byproducts, such as molasses or honeys, or directly from sugarcane juice, by a process of fermentation and distillation.

Tequila

a regionally specific distilled beverage and type of alcoholic drink made from the blue agave plant, primarily in the area surrounding the city of Tequila, 65 km (40 mi) northwest of Guadalajara, and in the highlands (Los Altos) of the central western Mexican state of Jalisco.

Vodka

A distilled beverage composed primarily of water and ethanol, sometimes with traces of impurities and flavorings.

Whiskey

A type of distilled alcoholic beverage made from fermented grain mash.
